DRAFT — pending counsel review. This page describes the privacy design of the platform. A formal privacy policy will replace it before launch.

The short version

Identity protection is the product. We collect what's needed to verify businesses, move money safely, and keep the marketplace honest — and we engineer so that your counterparties learn as little about you as you choose.

What we collect

Business verification details (legal name, registration, banking — handled by our payment processor), account and storefront information, listings and orders, on-platform messages (which are filtered for contact information), and standard technical logs. The live demo on this site stores its data only in your own browser and sends nothing to us.

What we never do

We never reveal the legal identity behind a storefront alias to another participant — reveal is always the account owner's explicit choice. We never sell participant data. Relationship-graph data (who is approved with whom) is confidential and never shared, exported, or used to advertise one participant to another's counterparties.

Payments

Payment and verification data is processed by our payment provider (planned: Stripe) under their own privacy terms; card and bank details never touch our servers.
