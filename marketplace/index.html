How it works

Select, don't inquire.

In broker-driven markets you inquire and wait while somebody shops their private contacts. On QuietLot the relationships are already provisioned onto the shelf: you see real offers with real prices and pick your counterparty — while everyone's identity stays behind a verified storefront alias.

🕸️

1 · Approval is everything

Nothing is public. Sellers approve brokers; sellers and brokers approve buyers. Every relationship carries its own terms — minimums, price floors, communication rules, identity reveal.

🎭

2 · Masked, not anonymous

Every business trades as a verified storefront alias with a real ratings history. The platform always knows who's behind the mask — counterparties don't, until the relationship owner chooses.

🔀

3 · Brokers stay protected

A broker's suppliers are invisible to that broker's buyers. Re-list a pallet as cases at your markup — your buyer sees your storefront, your price, your terms. The chain is never exposed.
